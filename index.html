My Experience In The Vancouver Film Industry

Move To Vancouver

I moved to Vancouver Feb 2018 from Edmonton. After having traveled around the world on cruise ships playing in the orchestras, I decided to put down roots here in Vancouver, and explore what the city had to offer. I've always been fascinated with movies and learned that the best way to get on set is to be a background actor.

Contact

Headshots

One of the most important aspects of being an actor is having fantastic headshots. These are what casting agencies use to determine who gets to audition. Those with professional headshots will typically have an advantage over those without. Vancouver has some fantastic photographers that specialize in doing headshots only for actors.

So Many Productions!

I have been on some pretty incredible film sets. One of the first was Supergirl. We were shooting a crazy scene downtown where the whole city was supposed to be crumbling down around us.

A lot of these shoots were very early in the morning, and you have to endure very long days, sometimes 15 hours. Boredom is the real culprit, as there can be a lot of waiting around to go to set. The best part of the day is easily all the free food.
